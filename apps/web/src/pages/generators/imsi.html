What is an IMSI?

An IMSI (International Mobile Subscriber Identity) is a unique identifier associated with a mobile subscriber. It is stored on the SIM card and used by mobile networks to identify users. An IMSI is up to 15 digits: MCC (3 digits) + MNC (2–3 digits) + MSIN (up to 10 digits).
