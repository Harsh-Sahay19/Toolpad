Privacy policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Toolpad ("we", "us", "our") operates toolpad.in. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and tools. We are committed to handling your data responsibly.

Information we collect

Usage data. We use Google Analytics to collect anonymised information about how visitors use the site — which pages are visited, how long sessions last, and which tools are used. This data contains no personally identifiable information and is aggregated for analysis only.

Input data. When you use formatting or conversion tools (JSON formatter, Base64, etc.), the data you paste into the tool is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. This data is never transmitted to our servers. Document conversion tools (DOCX to PDF) send your file to our server for processing and delete it immediately after conversion.

Generated data. All data generated by our tools (fake names, addresses, IMEI numbers, etc.) is fictitious and not derived from any real person's information. We do not store generated outputs.

Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics (_ga, _gid) — anonymised usage analytics. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out browser add-on.
  • Google AdSense — we use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show personalised ads based on your browsing history. See Google's privacy policy for details.
  • Toolpad preferences (toolpad-theme, toolpad-recent) — stored locally in your browser to remember your dark mode preference and recently used tools. These never leave your device.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on the site. AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's ad settings.

Third party links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites (GitHub, npm, documentation sites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

Data retention

We do not retain any user-submitted input data. Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months per Google's standard retention policy. Files submitted for document conversion are deleted from our servers immediately after processing.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR (EU), India's PDPB, or similar laws to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information directly, most requests can be fulfilled by clearing your browser cookies and local storage. For any privacy concerns, contact us at the address below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
